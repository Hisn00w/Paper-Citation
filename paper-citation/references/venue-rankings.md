## Ranking Legend

- **A+**: Top-tier (highest prestige)
- **A**: High-tier (very prestigious)
- **B**: Good-tier (well-respected)
- **C**: Recognized-tier (established venues)
- **SCI-Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4**: Science Citation Index quartiles
- **SSCI-Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4**: Social Sciences Citation Index quartiles
- **CSSCI**: Chinese Social Sciences Citation Index (南大核心)
- **北核**: Peking University Core Journals (北大核心)
